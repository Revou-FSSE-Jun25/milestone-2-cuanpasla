How to play
  • Choose a difficulty and optionally adjust the min/max range.
  • Click Start New Game.
  • Enter a number and click Guess (or press Enter).
  • Hints will tell you if the secret number is higher or lower.
  • Win by guessing the number before you run out of attempts.
